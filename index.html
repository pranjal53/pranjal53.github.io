Pranjal Bhatia

Postdoctoral Research Associate | Multi-Agent Robotics

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at UNSW Canberra @ ADFA, specializing in autonomous swarm intelligence for unmanned aerial systems (UAS). My research focuses on decentralized multi-agent control, developing real-time strategies for connectivity maintenance, collision avoidance, and formation coordination in complex environments. I have extensive experience in aerial robotics, with applications ranging from logistics and search-and-rescue to multi-robot coordination and autonomy.
