Pranjal Bhatia

Hi there! I am currently pursuing a PhD at the QUT Centre for Robotics and IIIT Delhi. My research focuses on developing decentralized control algorithms for multi-agent systems. During my PhD, I created a unified framework combining global connectivity maintenance, decentralized collision avoidance, and formation control using control barrier functions for real-time multi-agent systems. I am interested in creating safe autonomous systems for real-world applications.
